This is the GDI adv communications center.

This building is fully functional and textured.
For the comunication dish you should make an animated tile in leveleditor.
(Set the frames for it etc. You can find the framenumbers in renx)

Also you will have to place in the front door by created a mp_dr_o.
Dont forget to make the mct and place in on the lower floor in the small camber.

The meshprefix name for this building is gcm.

Use this building for your newmaps or mods.
